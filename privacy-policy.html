NeoVPN Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

1. Introduction

NeoVPN ("we", "the app") is a VPN client application that helps users establish secure and private internet connections. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
  • We do not log your browsing history or internet traffic.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not sell or share any user data with third parties.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or analytics that track individual users.

3. Camera Permission

The app requests camera access solely for scanning QR codes to import VPN server configurations. The camera is not used for any other purpose. No images or video are captured, stored, or transmitted.

4. Network and VPN Permissions

The app requires network permissions to establish VPN connections. All internet traffic routed through the VPN is encrypted and sent to the server chosen by the user. We do not inspect, log, or modify the content of your traffic.

5. Installed Apps Access

The app may request permission to query installed applications on your device. This is used exclusively for the per-app proxy feature, which allows you to choose which apps route traffic through the VPN. The list of installed apps is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted externally.

6. Local Storage

The app stores VPN configuration profiles and user preferences locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to any external server other than the VPN servers you configure.

7. Crash Reports

Anonymous crash reports may be collected to improve app stability. These reports contain only technical information about the error and do not include any personal data or browsing history.

8. Third-Party Services

VPN traffic is routed through servers configured by the user. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party VPN server providers. We recommend using trusted server providers.

9. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13 years of age.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
